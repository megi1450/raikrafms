About RAIKRA

RAIKRA Facility Management Solutions is a partner-led organization delivering integrated facility services with a strong focus on governance, compliance, and operational excellence.

What sets RAIKRA apart is its forward-thinking approach to smart facility management. By leveraging cutting-edge digital technologies, RAIKRA enables centralized, all-in-one control of assets without the need for physical proximity.

Through data-driven frameworks such as COBie (Construction Operations Building Information Exchange), traditional facility operations are transformed into a digital-first, intelligent ecosystem. This ensures real-time visibility, informed decision-making, and streamlined operations—enhancing efficiency, transparency, and long-term sustainability across diverse asset classes.
