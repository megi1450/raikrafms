COBie Data Integration

COBie is the foundation of structured facility data. It ensures that every asset handed over from construction is usable, traceable, and operationally relevant from day one.

RAIKRA converts COBie from a compliance document into a **living FM database** used daily by operations, maintenance, and audit teams.

What We Do

  • Capture COBie data directly from BIM models
  • Validate asset attributes and classifications
  • Align data with FM and CMMS platforms
  • Ensure lifecycle usability, not just handover

Why It Matters

  • No data loss between project and operations
  • Faster maintenance planning
  • Accurate asset registers
  • Audit and compliance readiness

Remote Asset Control

Remote Asset Control enables facilities to be monitored, diagnosed, and optimized without physical dependency on site presence.

RAIKRA’s remote control framework shifts FM from **reactive maintenance** to **predictive and preventive operations**.

Capabilities

  • Real-time HVAC, power & safety monitoring
  • Automated alerts for abnormal behavior
  • Remote diagnostics and adjustments
  • Energy performance tracking

Operational Impact

  • Reduced downtime
  • Lower operational costs
  • Improved response time
  • Extended asset lifespan

Compliance & Governance

Compliance is not an annual activity. It must be embedded into daily facility operations to reduce risk and build stakeholder confidence.

RAIKRA integrates statutory compliance, safety, and governance into **routine operational workflows**, not separate checklists.

Governance Framework

Business Value
